\Sir Ken Robinson makes an entertaining and profoundly moving case for creating
an education system that nurtures (rather than undermines) creativity.

Creativity expert Sir Ken Robinson challenged the way we educate our children,
championing a radical rethink of how our school systems cultivate creativity
and acknowledge multiple types of intelligence.
